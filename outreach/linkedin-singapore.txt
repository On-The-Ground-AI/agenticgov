The WEF released "Making Agentic AI Work for Government — A Readiness Framework" last month. Solid report, but mostly conceptual.

So I built what it would actually look like running in Singapore. 🇸🇬

agenticgov.vercel.app — a working visualisation with six interconnected agentic apps (Mission Control, TenderAI, FiscalAI, ReadinessMap, GovBench, TransparencyAI) covering 70 government functions. Real SG agencies wired in: GovTech, IMDA, MAS, MOH, MOE, MTI, MND, MOM, MSF, PSD. Real programmes: SkillsFuture, KidSTART, Smart Nation 2030, Singpass, MyInfo.

The interesting part isn't any single app. It's the cross-app flows — how a tender risk surfaces in TenderAI, propagates to FiscalAI, lands in Mission Control, and ends up scored on GovBench. That's where the readiness framework actually has teeth.

There's a live scan demo on the homepage if you want to see it move.

Would love your reaction — happy to talk through it with anyone in the public service who's wrestling with how to operationalise this.
